

1

Computers are amazing tools,

capable of feats we only dreamed of

in the past. They help us do our work

and keep us in touch with friends and family.

2

Computers have transformed the way

we live and the way we learn. They’re

essential for business and for school.

They’re also great for entertainment.

3

Computers are amazing machines,

capable of anything we can imagine.

With the power of the internet,

nothing is out of reach. We can explore

the world and learn new things, all from the

comfort of our own homes.

4

Computers are amazing tools,

and we can’t imagine living without them.

Thank you, computers, for making our lives

so much easier and more fun.
