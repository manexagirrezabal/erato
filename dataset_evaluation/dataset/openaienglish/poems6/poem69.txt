

The beautiful game,
football,
Is a simple sport
But so complex in its beauty.

A game of inches
And moments that can
Make or break a season.

For the players
It is a life's work
And for the fans
It is a religion.

The game is played
On fields of green
With 22 players
And a ball that is
Round.

It is a simple sport
But so complex in its beauty.
